Who am I ?

Me, Myself & I

My name is Sasha Kharoubi, borned and raised in Paris. I'm now living in Netanya, Israel.

I grew up with the computer world alongside with my father. Since my young age I always found the technology fascinating and it only got better at the very beggining of that mysterious new thing that came out INTERNET.

As a curious guy, I definitly wanted to understand it, test it, play with it, and even control it !

Design

Design always had a special place in my heart ! The love for beauty of the Architecture, Technology, Art, Sculpture, Symetry gave me special eyes.

It was obvious for me to find the Front-End position the more accurate for my personnality. Flat colors, Ergonomic use and Sleek design, is from this day my motto.

Making good looking and ergonomic design was a priority for me, even a real hobby !

Education

For my professional Education, I rapidly found the regular cursus at University not really my thing. I wanted to start practicing my passion and for that I needed something much shorter but nevertheless as hard.

Coding Bootcamp showed up for me as a great opportunity for me to test my capacities and to focus on the ultimate goal of working as a Front-End dev.
Developer's Institute gave me this opportunity!
