What does it do ?

Social

Creating a Bot Software can be for many differents usages, but the Social part was definitly the most amusing and fun choice to make. For that I chose Twitter wich like instagram have a really high demand for automation Bots.

Fast & Easy

When I had the idea for this project, I needed something easy and quick to use, not too many options to lose the average user. I then chose to let the user type his credentials and let him choose between Like or Retweet according to a picked topic for the user to choose.

Automation

A bot, as it's name tells, is basically a robot. A robot that will complete the tasks you asked for, and doing it in the most transparent way. My Twibot will like or retweet tweets by itself and wont stop until you choose to, it can run for an entire night for example !

What is it ?

Auto Like & Retweet for Twitter

I've always been fascinated by automation / cheats little softwares. So when I thought about a project to realize, a bot was the natural choice for me to make. I never tried a project like that and it was really exciting to go and try that ! I wanted something cool and actually usefull for a certain type of people.

Twibot was at first easy to build, Tkinter was pretty easy to understand for a new one, and I rapidly had a pretty cool GUI. But quicky I had to find the way of targetting likes and retweets according to a topic. I used Selelium for browser automation and it worked well ! After solving out the issues, seeing the program works as predicted was a real pleasure !

Techs Used : Python / Tkinter / Selenium
